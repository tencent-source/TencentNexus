Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TencentNexus. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our URL shortening service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • URLs: The long URLs you submit for shortening
  • Custom Aliases: Optional custom short codes you create

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Click Data: Number of times your short links are clicked
  • Timestamps: When links are created
  • Browser Storage: Links saved in your browser's localStorage

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide URL shortening services
  • Generate QR codes for your links
  • Track click statistics for your links
  • Maintain and improve our service

4. Data Storage

4.1 Browser LocalStorage Only

All shortened URLs are stored only in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Privacy-first: Links stay only on your device
  • No backend: Data is never transmitted to any server
  • No tracking: We cannot see or access your links
  • Full control: Clear your browser data anytime to delete everything
  • Device-specific: Links only work on the device where you created them

Important: Because data is stored locally:

  • Clearing browser data will delete all your links
  • Links won't sync across devices
  • Incognito/private browsing won't save links

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

5.1 GitHub Pages

  • For website hosting only (no data storage)
  • Subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy

5.2 Ko-fi

  • For donation/support functionality
  • Subject to Ko-fi's Privacy Policy

5.3 Google Analytics (if enabled)

  • For usage analytics
  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use localStorage (not cookies) to store your links locally. Third-party services (Ko-fi, Google Analytics) may use cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • No server-side storage of personal information
  • Client-side only architecture

Important: Since data is stored in public GitHub Issues, do not shorten sensitive or private URLs.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all GitHub Issues containing URL mappings
  • Delete: Remove links from your browser's localStorage
  • Clear Data: Clear your browser data at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International Users

Your data may be stored on servers located in various countries, including the United States, through GitHub's infrastructure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • GitHub: TencentNexus Repository
  • Creator: pinoyakogago

13. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union:

  • We process data based on legitimate interest
  • You have the right to data portability
  • You can request data deletion (clear localStorage, we can close GitHub Issues)
  • You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

14. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell your data)
